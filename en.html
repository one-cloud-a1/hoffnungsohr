Welcome to Hoffnungsohr

Hearing care near Cologne — in English

New to the Cologne area, or simply more comfortable in English? At Hoffnungsohr in Rösrath (Hoffnungsthal) you are in good hands. We are an owner-run master hearing care practice, just about 20 minutes from central Cologne, and we are happy to advise you in fluent English or German.

Whether you need a first hearing test, modern hearing aids, custom ear protection or in-ear monitors — we take the time to get it right, without corporate targets and without time pressure.

What we offer

Hearing aids

Modern hearing aids, individually fitted

Discreet, digital hearing systems from leading manufacturers — with Bluetooth streaming to your phone and TV. Fitted precisely to your ears with our Hoffnungsohr-Target method (real-ear measurement), not to average values.

Free hearing test

Clarity in about 15 minutes

A professional hearing test, free of charge and with no obligation. You will know straight away how well you hear — and whether a hearing aid would help.

Ear protection

Custom-made hearing protection

Tailor-made protection for work, music, motorsport, sleeping and water sports — comfortable and effective.

Costs & insurance

Hearing aids from €0 with statutory insurance

With a doctor's prescription, German statutory health insurance covers a fully digital hearing aid at no extra cost. We handle the billing for you. Privately insured patients receive a detailed invoice for their insurer.

Your hearing care professional

Johannes Penzel — personally, from start to finish

Hoffnungsohr is owner-run: there is no call centre and no rotating staff. Master hearing care professional Johannes Penzel personally carries out your hearing test, fits your hearing aids and looks after you long term — in English or German.

Our promise: honest advice, the latest technology and genuinely taking the time to listen. Because good hearing is about far more than a device — it is your connection to the people who matter to you.
